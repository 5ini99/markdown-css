Markdown.css

A peverse way to make your HTML look like markdown, purely via CSS.

Can’t get enough of raw markdown? Me neither. Despite looking like raw markdown, the text below actually comes from well-formed HTML. Just add markdown.css to your page and anything inside the class markdown will get “marked down.”
